What is SpongyBot?

SpongyBot is a multi-platform instant messaging bot which has a bunch of commands to 'enhance' your instant messaging experience.


What features does it have?

Lots.


What platforms is it currently on?

Skype and Discord. Slack, Telegram and IRC are coming soon!
